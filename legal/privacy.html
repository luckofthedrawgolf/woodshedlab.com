Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 23, 2025

1. What we collect

Basic contact details (like name and email), application details for instructors, and messages you send us. If you submit media, we process it to provide services.

2. How we use data

  • To respond to inquiries and process instructor applications
  • To improve our website and future services
  • To communicate important updates

3. Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We may use trusted providers (e.g., email/form processors) strictly to run the site. We’ll disclose data if required by law.

4. Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it.

5. Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us at Contact.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards. No method is 100% secure, but we aim to protect your information.

7. Children

Our services aren’t directed to children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll change the date above when we do.

9. Contact

Questions? Visit our Contact page.
